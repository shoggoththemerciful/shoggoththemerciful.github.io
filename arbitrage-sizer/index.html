A same-side spread uses high bid - low ask - fees. It only behaves like locked arbitrage if the sell, short, or inventory leg is actually available.

Math only. Confirm the contracts have the same resolution criteria, settlement timing, liquidity, fees, withdrawal costs, and rules before treating a spread as risk-free.
